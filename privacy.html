Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

PromptVault ("we", "us", or "operated by Danial") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit prompt.coursebag.com (the "Service").

Key Summary: We do not collect personal data directly. We do not use cookies. We do not have user accounts. The only data processing happens through third-party services described below when you interact with them.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this website is:

Danial
Website: prompt.coursebag.com
Contact: Via the links on our website

2. Information We Collect

We operate a static website with no backend server, no database, and no user accounts. We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal data.

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No account credentials or authentication data
  • No payment information (donations are handled externally)
  • No IP addresses (we do not log server traffic)
  • No browsing behavior or analytics
  • No cookies or local storage data

2.2 Information Processed by Third Parties

When you use certain features, third-party services may process data on your behalf:

2.2.1 Puter API (AI Prompt Generator)

What: When you use the "Generate Prompt" feature, the text you enter is sent to Puter's AI servers for processing.

Provider: Puter Inc. — puter.com

Data processed: The prompt description text you type into the generator.

Purpose: To generate an AI-crafted prompt based on your description.

Legal basis: Your explicit consent (you initiate the request by clicking "Generate Prompt").

Retention: Governed by Puter's Privacy Policy.

2.2.2 Google Fonts

What: We load fonts from Google Fonts CDN.

Provider: Google LLC — Google Privacy Policy

Data processed: Your IP address and browser user-agent may be logged by Google when fonts are requested.

Purpose: To display typography on the website.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (website presentation).

2.2.3 Buy Me a Coffee (Donations)

What: When you click the "Donate" button, you are redirected to Buy Me a Coffee's website.

Provider: Buy Me a Coffee — Buy Me a Coffee Privacy Policy

Data processed: Any information you provide on their platform (name, email, payment details) is processed solely by Buy Me a Coffee.

Purpose: To process voluntary donations.

Legal basis: Performance of a contract (donation transaction with Buy Me a Coffee).

Note: We do not receive or store any payment information from donations.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal data directly, we do not use personal data for any purpose. The third-party services listed above use data solely to provide their respective functions as described.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

Under the GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases for the limited data processing that occurs:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For the Puter AI prompt generator — you explicitly initiate the request.
  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)): For Google Fonts — necessary for website presentation.
  • Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b)): For donation processing — handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or a jurisdiction with equivalent data protection laws, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to request information about what personal data we hold about you. Since we do not store personal data, there is no data to disclose.

5.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data. Since we do not store personal data, this right does not apply to our operations.

5.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to Be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Since we do not store personal data, there is nothing to delete on our end. However, you may request deletion of data held by third-party services (Puter, Google, Buy Me a Coffee) by contacting them directly.

5.4 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format. Since we do not store personal data, this right does not apply to our operations.

5.5 Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest. You may do so by contacting us.

5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3))

Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time. You can stop using the AI prompt generator at any time.

5.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country of residence if you believe your data protection rights have been violated.

6. Data Transfers

Third-party services may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • Puter API: Servers may be located in the United States. Puter is responsible for ensuring adequate data transfer safeguards.
  • Google Fonts: Google processes data globally. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers.
  • Buy Me a Coffee: Processed on their platform's infrastructure.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Data processed by third parties is retained according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Puter Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

8. Cookies

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any similar technologies on this website. There are no analytics tools, advertising scripts, or social media widgets that set cookies.

If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide a cookie consent mechanism before any cookies are set.

9. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your data protection rights, or have a complaint, please contact us through the links available on our website or reach out via our project repository.

For GDPR-related requests: Please include your full name, a description of your request, and proof of identity. We will respond within 30 days as required by the GDPR.
